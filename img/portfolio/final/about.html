Mogo Pilates is a host studio for The Pilates Center of Boulder Colorado (TPC). TPC is known as one of the most rigorous classical Pilates training programs worldwide. It used to be that you had to move to Boulder to participate in the TPC training program. The TPC training program is now online and can be done remotely. Victoria is a TPC advisor which means that trainees can do their observed teaching hours, advisor meetings and some of their exams at Mogo Pilates. The trainees can also get their observation and lesson hours done at Mogo Pilates. Trainees travel to Boulder for their final performance and practical teaching exams.
